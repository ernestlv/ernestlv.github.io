Therapy

For some life is a mystery, for some is sex, drugs and alcohol to fill those voids carved by deep wounds.
Compulsive drinking, compulsive running are just a screen for the real sickness.
A sickness concocted in dreams and nocturnal nightmares, in sweaty nights, in moments of tears and sadness.
You hide in the mountains, you hide in the trails, you hide in the arms of other men but it always finds you because is within you.
You hide in drugs, you change your name and change the truth, because you are trying to hide from yourself. Dishonesty becomes a habit a way of existing.
The day becomes night and the night becomes day, there is no up and down, nor east or west.
You run instead of facing your action. Disappear in a mexican town or in fake transactions.
You value no kindness, nor pretty detail that a kiss entails.
loyalty means nothing. So you try to become nothing because you are afraid of who you are a wounded baby girl.
